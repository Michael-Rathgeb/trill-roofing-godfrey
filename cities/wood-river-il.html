How much does a roof patch job cost?

Asphalt shingle repairs are more affordable than slate or tile work. Wood River's labor rates are moderate compared to larger metro areas, keeping costs reasonable. What is the 25% rule in roofing?

The 25% rule states that if more than 25% of a roof's surface is damaged or deteriorated, full replacement is recommended over repair. Wood River building codes often use this threshold to determine permit requirements. For a 2,000 sq ft roof, 500+ sq ft of compromised shingles typically triggers a replacement recommendation instead of patching.

Will homeowners insurance cover roof repair?

Homeowners insurance in Illinois typically covers roof repairs from sudden, accidental events like storms, hail, wind, fire, or falling trees. Coverage generally excludes damage from age, wear and tear, neglect, or poor maintenance. Policy terms vary — some older roofs are covered at actual cash value rather than replacement cost, which includes depreciation. Review your specific policy to understand what applies.

Application requires specialized equipment and professional training to ensure proper coverage and effectiveness. DIY application can void any warranty.
